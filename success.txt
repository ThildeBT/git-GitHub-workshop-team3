Recipe for Success

Ingredients:

2 cups perseverance
1 cup clear goals (finely chopped)
3 tablespoons discipline
A pinch of curiosity
½ cup resilience
1 handful of patience
A dash of courage
Optional: support from others

Instructions:

Start by setting your goals in a large bowl. Be specific—vague ingredients lead to bland results.
Slowly mix in perseverance and discipline. Stir consistently; skipping steps can cause the mixture to fall apart.
Add curiosity and courage. These will help the recipe rise beyond expectations.
Fold in resilience, especially when the mixture gets tough or sticky. This step is essential.
Let everything simmer with patience. Do not rush—success needs time to develop its full flavor.
Taste as you go and adjust: add more effort if it feels lacking, or a bit more rest if it becomes overwhelming.
Serve daily with a side of reflection and gratitude.

Chef’s Note:
Success is best enjoyed fresh, but the recipe can be reused and refined over time.

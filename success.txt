Recipe for Success

Ingredients:

2 cups perseverance
1 cup clear goals (finely chopped)
3 tablespoons discipline
A pinch of curiosity
½ cup resilience
1 generous handful of patience
A dash of courage
1 strong, reliable support network (essential, not optional)

Instructions:

Begin by placing your goals in a large bowl. Define them clearly—precision gives the recipe direction.
Stir in perseverance and discipline, mixing steadily to build a solid base.
Add curiosity and courage to bring energy and depth to the mixture.
Gently fold in resilience, especially when the texture becomes difficult—this prevents everything from breaking apart.
Now, incorporate your support network. Let their encouragement, advice, and perspective blend throughout the mixture; this step strengthens the entire structure and keeps it from collapsing under pressure.
Allow the mixture to simmer with patience. Check in regularly, but don’t rush the process.
Taste and adjust along the way—lean on your support network when the flavour feels off or uncertain.

Chef’s Note:
This recipe thrives when shared. A strong support network not only enhances the flavour but helps you keep cooking, even when the heat is high.

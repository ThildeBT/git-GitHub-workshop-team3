CARROT CAKE (optionally vegan)

4.5 dl finely grated carrot
3.5 dl farin sugar (or brown), pressed tightly to the measure cup (if brown sugar, no needed to press)
2 dl oil
1.5 dl milk (soy milk, if vegan) or water
5.5 dl wheat flour
0.5 dl potato flour
1.5 tsp baking powder
1.5 teaspoons of baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon cloves
Frosting:

100 g margarine (vegan option) OR butter
225 g unflavored cream cheese (soy cream cheese if vegan)
2 dl powdered sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla sugar
(almond flakes)
Preheat the oven to 175 degrees.
Grease the cake pan with margarine and roll the pan in wheat flour until the inside of the pan is evenly floured.
Measure brown sugar, oil and soy milk into a bowl. Mix.
In another bowl, mix the dry ingredients and add them to the sugar mixture. Stir in the grated carrot.
Pour the batter into a floured pan (diameter approx. 22-24 cm) and bake for about 45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
Let cool.
Mix the frosting ingredients together and spread it over the cake. Decorate the cake with slivered almonds.

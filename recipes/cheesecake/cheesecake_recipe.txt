# Step-by step 

Prep & Crust: Preheat oven

Mix crumbs, sugar, and butter. Press into the bottom and 1 inch up the sides of a 9-inch springform pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then cool.

Make Filling: Beat softened cream cheese and sugar together on medium speed until smooth and creamy (do not overmix). Add sour cream and vanilla.

Add Eggs: Turn mixer to low and add eggs one at a time, mixing just until combined.

Water Bath Setup: Wrap the outside of the springform pan securely with heavy-duty foil. Pour filling over the crust. Place the pan inside a larger, shallow roasting pan and fill the outer pan with 1 inch of hot water.

Bake: Bake for 60-70 minutes until edges are set but the center still has a slight jiggle.

Cool & Chill: Turn off the oven, crack the door, and let the cake sit for 1 hour. Remove, let it cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 6-8 hours or overnight.
